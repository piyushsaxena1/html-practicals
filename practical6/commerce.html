Department of Commerce

Department of Commerce, Ramanujan College was established in the year 1964, with B.A. Commerce (now B.Com). Then, in 1969, Bachelor in Commerce [B.Com. (H)] was also introduced by the department. The department has resourceful & efficient faculty and is the largest department with highest student strength in the college. The department not only covers the commerce courses but also caters to the needs of the students of B. A. (Prog.) by offering discipline courses in Accounting and Finance and Entrepreneurship and Small Business (ESB).
